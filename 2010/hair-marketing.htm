On this page you’ll find a selection of marketing materials for the tribal love-rock musical Hair to help you promote the show to all your friends, family, work colleagues and even strangers! They’ll be more coming over the following weeks, but if there’s anything you think would be useful then please let Stephen Beeny know by sending him an email on webmaster@sedos.co.uk!

If you want a hard copy of the poster (A4 or A3) printed out for your place of work or local shop, bar or front window then please let us know and we’ll print some out for you.

A5 flyers are available at rehearsals.

A6 postcards will be available in the next couple of weeks.
