News Update

First off, welcome to our new website!! We hope it is a bit easier to navigate than the last one, and should serve as a one stop shop for everything sedos.If you have any comments or suggestions about the site, please email me at press@sedos.co.uk.

2007 certainly finished with a bang as Sweeney Todd stormed the Bridewell to a sell out audience and absolutely fantastic reviews. It was an incredibly strong finish to an incredibly strong year and huge congratulations to everyone involved.

2008 looks to have started in a similar fashion as everyone is busier than ever. They said it couldn't be done, but Chris DePury and Matt Gould did in fact stage an excellent production of The Hired Man in just two days at Wiltons music hall. It was a fantastic weekend, both hard work and fun, and general consensus was that we should definately do it again, so watch this space!

As we heave into March things are busier than ever. With rehearsals for Red Hot and Black and ROAD well underway, the time has come for the cast to start chivvying the troops into buying their tickets. If you haven't bought yours yet - Shame on You! and click here right away to rectify the situation! Tickets are going quickly, and for those that get in quick there are a limited amount of cabaret tables still available for Red Hot and Black.

My Favorite Year is all cast now, so congratualtions to everyone involved. A full cast list can be found on the noticeboard, but Andrew assures me that the standard was as ever, incredibly high, so watch this space to find out when you can get your tickets.

Registration for auditions is also underway for Rosencrantz and Guildenstern are dead <link to auditions page> and The sedos Edinburgh tour, Don Carlos - This is an open audition, but for a taste of what both plays are all about, and to grill our esteemed directors Marchie and Becky, there is a workshop on the 22nd of April. More information is on the auditions page.

And last, but not least, the time for the AGM is nearly upon us, so keep the 23rd of April free, if only to see Will stumble through the 2007 round up after a couple of glasses of wine. More detailed information in on the noticeboard

Other than that, have a good time rehearsing/auditioning/performing/socialising and see you all at the Bridewell!
