Sponsor Us



Rooted as we are in the Square Mile, with many of our members drawn from City firms, Sedos is pleased to offer companies a variety of opportunities to sponsor us.

Sponsorship is vital to the future of the society, and will augment our support for our chosen charity, Half Moon Theatre. 2010 promises to be the most prolific of many high-profile years for Sedos, and as such should offer great publicity opportunities for our supporters.

Support can be big or small, and can take the form of sponsorship for the entire 2010 season, headline sponsoring of a specific production (such as Allsop LLP and Resolution Property plc for Grand Hotel in 2007 and The Bank of New York for Jekyll & Hyde in 2005); or sponsorship of an aspect of a production such as the lighting, costumes or set; or simply buying ad space in our programme.

In return for your support we’ll be delighted to offer you tailored corporate performances, with profits going to a charity of your choice; publicity on our posters, flyers, website and in our programmes, early or preferential booking for a performance of your choice, bespoke cabaret teams for your events to perform numbers from sponsored shows, workshop opportunities for your staff, their children or children from a charity you support; or even an in-house performance at your office of one of our 'Shakespeare in the City' tours, exclusively for staff, clients and friends.

Sedos is proud to have enjoyed the support of many City organisations including Herbert Smith, The Investor Relations Society, London Stock Exchange, Winterflood Securities, Execution, The Bank of New York, Merrill Lynch, ABN Amro, Swiss Re, RD:IR, Accenture, Citigroup, Allsop LLP and Resolution Property PLC.


For more information about getting involved in partnering with Sedos in a production, please email sponsorship@sedos.co.uk.
