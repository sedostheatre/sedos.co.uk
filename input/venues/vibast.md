## Vibast Community Centre

Vibast is used as a venue for Sedos workshops, auditions and rehearsals.

## Your visit to Vibast Community Centre

**Address**

Vibast Community Centre\
167 Old Street\
London \
EC1V 9NH

**Getting to Vibast Community Centre**

Vibast is very near Old Street station, with Barbican, Moorgate, Liverpool Street, Farringdon, Shoreditch High Street and Angel also among its closest stations.

**Accessibility**

Vibast Community Centre has level access and an accessible toilet.
