## King Square Community Centre

Sedos holds workshops, auditions and rehearsals at King Square Community Centre.

## Your visit to King Square Community Centre

**Address**

King Square Community Centre\
Blackwell House\
Pankhurst Terrace\
London\
EC1Y 8DY

**Getting to King Square Community Centre**

The closest stations are Angel, Barbican, Old Street and Farringdon.

**Accessibility**

King Square Community Centre has level access and an accessible toilet. The self-opening door to the venue is currently broken.
