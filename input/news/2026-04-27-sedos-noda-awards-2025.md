28 April 2026

**Sedos has won four National Operatic and Dramatic Association (NODA) district awards for our 2025 shows.**

Our winners are:

**The Molly Harle Award for best female character in a musical:** Sarah Hunt as Violet in *[Violet](https://www.sedos.co.uk/shows/2025-violet)*

**The Sybil Conway Award for best female character in a play:** Jamila Jennings-Grant as Hedda Gabler in *[Hedda Gabler](https://www.sedos.co.uk/shows/2025-hedda-gabler)*

**The Joe Putnam Award for best production of a musical:** *[Dreamgirls](https://www.sedos.co.uk/shows/2025-dreamgirls)*

**The NODA London Flame Awards for inspirational contribution to the pursuit of excellence in theatre and enhancement of the overall theatrical experience:** Sedos for the lighting in *[Dreamgirls](https://www.sedos.co.uk/shows/2025-dreamgirls)*, designed by Ben Sassoon

Huge congratulations to Sarah, Jamila, Ben, everyone involved in our production of *Dreamgirls*, and all who contributed to making our 2025 season such a success.
