Why do yoga?

It's good for you - Physically, mentally and spiritually. Why believe us? Well, Yoga is thousands of years old, and it has a pretty cool list of benefits. At it's core yoga looks at a union of movement, breath and mind, which is really good for your overall health.

Physical – Can help relieve day to day tensions, stretches and strengthens muscles, helps flexibility and balance.

Mental – De-stress and relieve anxieties, refocus your mind, and rise to a challenge.

The above list is just a few things. It's no silver bullet, but there is plenty to gain from Yoga practice.


What is Vinyasa Yoga?
Vinyasa yoga is a modern practice that links postures together with breath in a flowing sequence. Expect a flow that is never the same twice, the origins are in Ashtanga but Vinyasa flow practice is a freer style that helps build heat and then chill you.


FAQs

"Do I have to chant?"
No. There is a time and a place for chanting and ommming, this is not one of them. The class will be taught in english and won't involve chants or ommms.

"I've never done yoga before, will this class be ok for me?"
Everyone starts somewhere, the class will be tailored to those that show up, and postures have versions that work for all levels, from first-timers to long-timers.

"I'm not flexible enough/I can't touch my toes"
Don't worry about it, flexibility and strength are part of the process in yoga. There are adaptations for most things.

“I have an injury, can I still attend”
Drop Matt a line before the class, or if it a small issue let him know before class starts.

Practice is the key word. No one can “finish” Yoga, it’s about practising. So whether you are a keen yogi, or never tried it this class is suitable for you.
