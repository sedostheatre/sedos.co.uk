Archive

With a history of over a hundred years of London theatre, the Sedos archives are full to bursting. In a box, in an attic somewhere in Kent, are hundreds of show programmes, press cuttings and photos of some of the theatrical delights Sedos has brought the City of London during the last century.

As we stumble into the digital age, however, we are also keen to keep more readily accessible records of the various shows Sedos have produced. For information on our more recent exploits, click the links to the right for a run down on our past seasons.

If you have been involved with a Sedos show over the last hundred years, please email us with your memories (and any photos you might have!) at webmaster@sedos.co.uk.
