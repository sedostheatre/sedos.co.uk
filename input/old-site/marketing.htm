Branding

We're trying to build the Sedos brand and make it more consistent. The guidelines in the Communications Document, templates and downloads below allow us to achieve this.

  • The 'L' should be used on the front of show promotional material (eg postcards, posters and flyers)
  • The next show promotion box should be used on the flyer back.
  • 'Resident' tag and Social Media links should be included on the flyer back.
  • Our strapline is 'The City of London's premiere amateur theatre company'

  • Web addresses: It is the house style to NOT use the ‘www’ in all web addresses. For example, it should be ‘sedos.co.uk’, ‘facebook.com/sedostheatre’, ‘twitter.com/sedos’, ‘youtube.com/sedosvideo’, flickr.com/sedos’ and ‘bridewelltheatre.org

Sedos Flyer Template

The templates were created to give designers a starting point so they don't feel like they're starting from scratch - a level of creative interpretation is obviously allowed. They also put all the relevant info in one place and provide the details to ensure that we cross-market our channels (fb, twitter etc) and future shows on flyers.

  • Front Template - Where possible the template 'L' dimensions would be preferred but can be adapted for creative reasons (eg Cwowsa, Denmark both sedos.org.uk/2009) or that the horizontal may be increased dependent on show requirements (eg The Wild Party -  sedos.co.uk/2009 where musicals have more info to include)

  • Back Template - the back template for the bottom of the flyer back should be used as the preferred option, but the design can be modified as required as long as the info is all included. Please include the next show promotion box.

  • Placing of information - with a double sided flyer there is flexibility as to what information is on the front and back as long as it's all there somewhere however the Sedos logo and URL (minus the www) should always be in the ’L’ on the front and the poster should have all the key details on a single side. If there isn't a separate poster then the flyer front should include all the key details.
