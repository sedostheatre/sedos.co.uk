Christmas carols and party

Sign up for Christmas Carols and Christmas Party!

We're joining forces with our friends at Geoids and Centre Stage for carolling under the Christmas tree at Trafalgar Square in aid of Crisis – the homelessness charity. We’d love for you to come and sing in our impromptu choir – all abilities are welcome!

After carolling we‘ll be heading towards the Bridewell Theatre Bar for our (potentially a Christmas first!) joint London Drama Societies Christmas Party.

Let us know that you'll be joining our carolling choir by signing up here When: Saturday 16th December - 3.30-5pm Where: under the big Christmas Tree in Trafalgar Square

You'll need to be on the guest list for our Christmas Party by signing up hereWhen: Saturday 16th December - 6.30pm til late Where: Bridewell Theatre Bar - guest list entry only!
