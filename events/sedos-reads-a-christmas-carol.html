Sedos Reads: A Christmas Carol

Arguably Charles Dickens most famous novel, and certainly one of the best known Christmas stories, this lively theatrical adaptation is by director Peter Foster following the success of The Miller and his Men. 

If you would like to be part of our audience for A Christmas Carol, we have up to 85 slots, available on a first come, first served basis. Book early to avoid disappointment! Email christmas_carol@sedos.co.uk to request a spot.
